Interests

3D Printing

I enjoy messing around my 3D printer at home when I have a chance. It is based off a cheap kit found on the internet that I bought back in the summer of 2016, but it has served me well and I have been able to use to print some subjectively cool things such as USB flash drive holders, phone stands, in-game character models and was also used for the hinge for a dish rack on one of my IPRO projects.

Baking

I really enjoy baking whenever I can. It started off with those premade mixes and store bought ready-to-bake dough, but after a few runs I realized it would probably be better to start from scratch. Armed with the internet and the limitless amount of recipes you can find online, I started with a classic: the chocolate chip cookie.

Not half bad, huh? It tasted pretty good too. I was pretty satisfied with the result. On the other hand, once you start baking and you realize it's pretty fun, there's no point stopping at plain old chocolate chip cookies (even though they were pretty awesome). I even tried to do that fancy little fruit garnish on the side with that chocolate swirl.
